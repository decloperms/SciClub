About

The CoEP Science club, nurturing the innovation, raising the profile of science through additional scientific activities has managed to conjure up a lot of interest amongst students of CoEP.

Science rules the world; we the mankind just figure it out by making laws and theories. Since birth of mankind he is trying to explain the phenomenon happening in the world with help of science.

Integrating technical skill with scientific theories, the club has initiated many projects, some of which being the construction of Microbial Fuel Cell, Project on Special and General Relativity, Quantum Mechanics etc.

The club strives to quench a student’s thirst for Science by organizing various exciting events such as Developing Demonstration Experiments, Do It Yourself Laboratories, Science Quiz(a quiz to test people’s knowledge on everything in the field of Science), Talks and Lectures By Eminent Scientists from all over the globe, Visits To Various Labs, Workshops and much more.

In addition to this, the club is heading towards having a collaboration with Tata Institute for Fundamental Researches (TIFR) and the Inter-University Centre for Astronomy and Astrophysics, Pune (IUCAA), with help of Dr. Yashwant Waghmare.

Discussions and brainstorming sessions are also held every week wherein members explore everything under the Sun.
